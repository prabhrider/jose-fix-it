Services

Hi, I am Jose Mujica. I am your local handyman in Chicago northwest suburbs. I should have a remedy for all of your house problems, after all, I am a handyman! Here is a list of things I can build or fix that I can think of the top of my head.

Kitchen

Whether you are looking into fully upgrade your kitchen or just add a few quick touch-ups to jazz up your kitchen, Jose got it all. I can do almost any job requirement to get your dream kitchen into a reality.

  • Install & repair cabinets
  • Add & paint crown molding
  • Install new tiles/wooden flooring
  • Add new sink & faucets
  • Add backsplash & under cabinet lights
  • Install new countertops

Deck

Do you have a deck or gazebo that has endured weather damage? Whether you are looking into restoration or adding a new porch, deck or gazebo. Jose can help you with enriching your backyard and make the most out of your outdoor place.

  • Add new custom deck & gazebo
  • Deck wood repair & staining
  • Add new pergola & screen porch
  • Repair or add new railing & stairs
  • Add or repair deck lighting

Basement

Want to finish your basement with a makeover and make it into a beautiful, livable space for the entire family? Consider the following services that I can perform:

  • Add new tile or wooden flooring
  • Repair, insulate & finish the drywall
  • Add or repair drop ceiling
  • Installing new lighting fixtures
  • Wiring & hanging home theatre
  • Install bar cabinets and countertops

Electrical

If you are looking for electrical services beyond your capabilities, you don't need to look elsewhere. Jobs I specialize in:

  • Light fixture, outlets & switches installation
  • Installing recessed lighting
  • Upgrading electrical panels & sub-panels
  • Installing & wiring for backup generators
  • Installing GFCIs & electrical surge protectors

Misc
